# Commander AI Grounding Steering

Commander AI is core from v1.1 onward.

Commander AI must be grounded in Commander data, source authority, cases, entities, baselines and audit records. It must not invent estate facts.

Outputs must be traceable, reviewed where action-impacting, and logged as Commander execution records.

AI may draft, explain, summarise, recommend and help navigate. It must not silently execute external writes, bypass approval chains or override baseline authority.
